About Me

Hi, I'm David Obando, a Computer Engineering student at ITCR specializing in cybersecurity, coding, and FOSS technologies. I'm pursuing CE with plans to become a cybersecurity analyst, currently studying information security independently alongside my formal education.

This site serves as my digital workshop for sharing technical projects, documenting my learning journey in CS and cybersecurity, and contributing to the open-source community. You'll find my work on security analysis, programming projects, and technical experiments here.

Beyond tech, I'm passionate about music production and composition, language learning, and exploring how technology intersects with creative expression. I believe in continuous learning and knowledge sharing within the tech community.

Feel free to explore my projects and reach out if you'd like to collaborate!
